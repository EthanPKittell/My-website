My Work History:

SubCom Test Software Engineering Internship

(Summers of 2022 and 2023)

During my internship, I mainly worked with C and C++. In this position, I wrote many instrument drivers in Labwindows/CVI for various forms of National Instruments fiber optic testing equipment. I also wrote many graphical user interfaces (also called GUIs) for helping operators work with test equipment. An example of one GUI I made was for verifying a device under test (or DUT) is set up correctly at a test set. While working at SubCom I also automated tests for setting up test sets and working with DUTs using TestStand and Labwindows/CVI. I also worked on documentation of setting up test sets, shop instructions for multiple pieces of software, and Visio diagrams for electrical work.
